My cats are awesome

Just like the mother of dragons, I have 3 babies that I didn't give birth to. One of them was planned, the other two just choose us.

Anastasia

She is the oldest, she has been with me since February 27, 2007. When I got her I was still living with my parents, so after I convince them that I could take proper care of pet, I found a posting that show her and her siblings after being drop at some girl front yard.

Fedora

She found me on December 29, 2009. I was still living with my parents and they didn't let me have more cats, so I called my boyfriend (now my husband) asking for help. I think he immediately told me that he would adopt her. That same night he went to my house to pick her up and it was love at first sight.

Bartolomeo

I found him on March 7, 2014. He was abandoned outside of an old house that was on my way to work. This time I was living with my husband and when I called him to tell him that I have found a baby cat (he was tiny, malnourished and sick) he was mad, but when I arrive to our apartment, he fall in love again.
